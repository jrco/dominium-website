The history behind Dominium
It's the year 3000, corporations are working on a new project, a machine that mimics human behaviour.

They need to gather information about people and use it to improve their creation and to do so they need to control the communication towers.

Ordinary people start to realize their privacy and rights are in jeopardy and so a group is built to fight against corporation intentions, the Insurgents.

Insurgents and Corporations now fight for the control of communication towers in a battle where only one can have their way.

Roles

Choosing the appropriate role it's the first step to win.
  • Attacker

Attacker

The attacker class are the front runners of the team, being able to capture points faster than the other classes and have the ability to burst capture a point.

  • Defender

Defender

The defender class are able to control the field, being able to increase the health of the points when defending from enemy team attacks.

  • Support

Support

The support class help control the teams energy consumption, being able to replenish the energy of they´re team members.
