Welcome to Dominium


Experience Dominium



Gather Your Friends

Join your friends and go outside! You will need to build a team with people you trust, choose your allies wisely because they will be helping you win.

Choose Your Color

Dominium allows you to customize your team as you wish. The color you choose will define your team so be sure to pick the right one.

Select Your Role

Choosing the role defines the initial strategy of your team. Discuss with teammates and decide what game strategy you will follow.

Enjoy Dominium

The most important thing: be sure to enjoy playing! Dominium is an entertaining game so be sure to live the total Dominium Experience.



Roles

Choosing the appropriate role is the first step to victory.

Attacker

The attacker class are the front runners of the team, being able to capture points faster than the other classes and have the ability to burst capture a point.

Defender

The defender class are able to control the field, being able to increase the health of the points when defending from enemy team attacks.

Support

The support class help control the teams energy consumption, being able to replenish the energy of their team members.
